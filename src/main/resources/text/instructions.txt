The goal of the game is to
avoid colliding with the
walls and the ground.

The player can jump by
pressing the space bar.
Otherwise, the tiger will
fall and hit the floor.

You can collect boar and
lamb to get extra points.

Boar - 3 Points
Lamb - 5 Points

If you collect a bottle of
wine, you enter god mode
and the gap between walls
will be bigger.
